Who We Are

Bridging property seekers and property owners.

Solven Realities Real Estate is a trusted property solutions company in Uganda, built on reliable, transparent, client-focused service.

Our Story

Property ownership, made simple and secure.

At Solven Realities, we make property ownership simple, secure and rewarding. Based in Uganda, we connect clients to the finest residential, commercial and investment properties with professionalism and integrity.

Whether you're buying, selling or renting, our team ensures a seamless experience from start to finish — and for property owners, our management services mean the relationship doesn't end at closing.

Every engagement is built around one principle: transparency. You should always know exactly where things stand, whether you're closing a sale or checking on a tenant.
