Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Solven Realities UG Limited ("Solven Realities," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal data you share with us through this website, in line with Uganda's Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our contact form or otherwise reach out to us, we may collect:

  • Your full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Details of the property or service you're inquiring about
  • Any other information you choose to include in your message

We do not collect payment information, national ID numbers, or other sensitive personal data through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about a property or service
  • Arrange viewings, consultations or follow-up communication
  • Maintain records of client communication for service quality

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data to third parties.

3. How We Store & Protect Your Information

Inquiries submitted through our contact form are delivered directly to our company email and are not stored in a public or third-party database beyond standard email delivery. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Form delivery service — to route contact form submissions to our email
  • Google Maps — to display our office location
  • Image hosting (Unsplash) — for property photography displayed on the site

These services may process limited technical data (such as IP address) as part of standard web delivery, governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

Under the Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, where we have no legal or business obligation to retain it
  • Withdraw consent to be contacted at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

6. Cookies

This website does not currently use tracking or advertising cookies. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and a cookie notice will be added.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
