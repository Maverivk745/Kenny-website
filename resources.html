Common Questions

Buying, selling & renting FAQs.

How do I start the process of buying a property?

Reach out with your budget, preferred location and property type. We'll share matching listings and arrange viewings for anything that fits.

What documents do I need to sell my property?

Typically the title deed, proof of ownership and any prior survey documents. We'll confirm exactly what's needed once we know your property type.

Do you handle rentals as well as sales?

Yes — we manage both sides of the rental process, from listing a property to matching and screening tenants.

Can you manage a property after I buy it?

Yes. Many clients move from a purchase directly into a management agreement, so the property is looked after from day one.

I live outside Uganda — can I still buy or invest?

Yes, this is common. We work with diaspora clients regularly, including coordinating viewings, paperwork and management remotely.

What areas do you cover?

Primarily Kampala and the immediately surrounding districts. Get in touch if your property is further out and we'll confirm coverage.

Still have questions?

Send us a message and we'll get back to you directly.
